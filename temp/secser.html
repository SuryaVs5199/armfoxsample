DEVELOPING SECURITY CRITICAL SOFTWARE REQUIRES SPECIAL EXPERTISE

Developing security critical software is not a task you can give to any software company. Security requirements need to be truly understood and taken into account in development right from the beginning. Throughout the life cycle of the software, new threats and needs keep raising, increasing the importance of good maintenance and support services.
