Key Highlights of Our Internal Medicine Billing Services

Continuous Training

All our internal medicine billing experts are rigorously trained to ensure that they are equipped with the skills necessary to handle changes in the medical industry as they happen, which helps minimize adverse impacts on our client’s business.

Customized Reports

Along with the standard weekly, monthly and quarterly reports that are offered by standard internal medicine billing companies, we also provide custom-built reports that provides a summary of financial performance over the past couple of months, quarters or years.

Comprehensive Security

We manage claims related data in a secure environment, which facilitates easy access to records. Our company also uses internal controls, which ensures that the claim progresses without any hindrance as part of a smooth flowing revenue stream.

Minimal Rejections

Our internal medicine billing services are renowned to deliver the lowest rejection rate in the industry. The credit goes to the system of checks and balances that we have placed along with a process of scrubbing, which ensure claims are paid without undue effort on part of the client.

Superior Quality Customer Support

Our round-the-clock customer service provide patients with all the help pertaining to their accounts and ensures that they have a seamless and courteous experience.

Rigorous AR Follow-up

Aging claims are rigorously followed-up on in a streamlined fashion to ensure that payments are collected within specified time frames.

Accurate and Timely Billing

Claims are generated and collections are processed in a prompt and accurate fashion to ensure that payments due from patients as well as the insurance carriers are received without any delays.
