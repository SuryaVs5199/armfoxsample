Optimized Pathology Billing Services for Reliable Outcomes

Arm Fox  has wide experience in providing pathology billing services to healthcare practices of all sizes. Over the years we have attained great familiarity with diverse fields of pathology - from general, anatomical and clinical to oral, maxillofacial, forensic, psycho and molecular pathology — which enables us to deliver efficient pathology medical billing services that will ensure delays, denials and revenue loss is a long-forgotten memory. Our streamlined approach allows for more aggressive claims follow-up and collection activity to ensure that you collect maximum reimbursements.

Key Highlights of Our Pathology Medical Billing Services

Accurate and On Time Billing

Claims are generated and collections are processed in a prompt and accurate fashion to ensure that payments due from patients as well as the insurance carriers are received without any delays.

Efficient Follow Up of Aged Claims

Aging claims are rigorously followed-up on in a streamlined fashion to ensure that payments are collected within specified time frames.

Superior Customer Service

Our round-the-clock customer service provide patients with all the help pertaining to their accounts and ensures that they have a seamless and courteous experience.

Very Low Rejection Rates

Our pathology medical billing services are renowned to deliver the lowest rejection rate in the industry. The credit goes to the system of checks and balances that we have placed along with a process of scrubbing, which ensure claims are paid without undue effort on part of the client.

Secure Processes

We manage claims related data in a secure environment, which facilitates easy access to records. Our company also uses internal controls, which ensures that the claim progresses without any hindrance as part of a smooth flowing revenue stream.

Standard and Custom-Built Reports

Along with the standard weekly, monthly and quarterly reports that are offered by standard pathology medical billing companies, we also provide custom-built reports that provides a summary of financial performance over the past couple of months, quarters or years.

Rigorous Training

All our pathology billing experts are rigorously trained to ensure that they are equipped with the skills necessary to handle changes in the medical industry as they happen, which helps minimize adverse impacts on our client’s business.
